# Smart Building

---

### Opening Your Space

1. **Open Space URL**: In your workspace, click on your organization’s URL slug name
2. **Access Space Site**: The space site will open automatically
   - You can preview the default 3D model without signing in
   - You can also click “_Change 3D Building File_” to upload and preview your own model
     <DocImageFrame>
       <img
         src="https://d33et8skld5wvq.cloudfront.net/images/spacedf-docs/onboarding/unauth_change_model.gif"
         alt="Sign In/Sign Up Your Space"
       />
     </DocImageFrame>
3. **Sign In**: Choose “Get Started” to sign up or sign in
   - Select **Sign Up** if you don’t have an account
   - Select **Sign In** if you already have an account
   - Use the same email/password or Gmail account from the organization site
     <DocImageFrame>
       <img
         src="/images/onboarding/signin-signup-your-space.gif"
         alt="Sign In/Sign Up Your Space"
       />
     </DocImageFrame>

> 👉 **Note**: If you don’t have a 3D model yet, you can use our MatterSpace app to scan your building and generate one. You can download a trial build via TestFlight [here](https://testflight.apple.com/join/KvV3U5DU). Follow the step-by-step guide below to create and export your model.

### Scan & Export 3D Model with MatterSpace

1. **Open MatterSpace App**: Launch the app on your mobile device
2. **Start Scanning**: Tap “Scan 3D Now”
3. **Scan Your Space**:
   - Move your phone slowly around the area
   - Point the camera up and down to capture walls, edges, and corners
   - Walk through the space and scan continuously as you move
4. **Track Scanning Progress**:
   - White lines will appear to show detected surfaces
   - A 3D model will be generated in real-time as you scan
5. **Complete Scanning**: Tap “Done Scanning” when finished
6. **Export Model**: Tap “Export Model” to download the 3D file to your device
